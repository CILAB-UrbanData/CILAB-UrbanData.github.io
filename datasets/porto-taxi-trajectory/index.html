The dataset covers one full year (2013-07-01 to 2014-06-30) and supports research on trajectory mining, destination prediction, and urban mobility analysis.

Dataset Description

This dataset contains taxi trajectories collected in the city of Porto, Portugal. Each row represents one completed taxi trip, including identifiers and temporal metadata as well as the full trajectory encoded as a polyline (a list of GPS points).

The timeline spans from 2013-07-01 to 2014-06-30, totaling 1,710,670 trips. It is commonly used for spatio-temporal modeling tasks such as destination prediction, travel time estimation, and trajectory mining.

The official source for downloads and terms is Alibaba Cloud Tianchi (dataset ID 94216).
